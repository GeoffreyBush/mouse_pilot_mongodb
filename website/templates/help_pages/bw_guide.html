How to view the Breeding Wing dashboard

On this page is a graphical view of the breeding cages. The breeding cages are distinguished by their colour:

The Grey colour indicates the cage is empty.

The Orange colour indicates the cage is currently housing the parent mice.

The Green colour indicates the parent mice are removed and the cage is currently housing only the litter.

To view the details of each cage, simply click the ID of the cage to open its own page:

The function to add and edit cages can also be navigated from this page.
